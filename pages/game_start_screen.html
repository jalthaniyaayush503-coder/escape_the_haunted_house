Game Instructions

🎮 Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move
  • SPACE or W/↑ - Jump

🎯 Objective

  • 🗝️ Collect golden keys to unlock doors
  • 🚪 Reach the door to advance to next level
  • 🏃‍♂️ Complete all levels to escape!

⚠️ Dangers

  • 👻 White ghosts patrol horizontally
  • 🔺 Red spikes cause damage
  • 💀 Falling objects from above
  • ❤️ You have 3 lives - use them wisely!

✨ Power-ups

  • ✝️ Cross power-up grants temporary ghost protection
  • 🛡️ Glowing effect indicates active protection
